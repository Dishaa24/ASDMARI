About Us

ASDMARI established in 2012 at Hooghly, West Bengal, India. The Founder Shihan Swaprakash Roy started with a small Karate Class at Babuganj Boys Club in his vicinity with only 3 students. In 2014 he collaborated with NSKA India as NSKA West Bengal. ASDMARI provides training on the art of unarmed combat in which systematic training of arms and legs defends any attack from assailants. Beside Karate (Traditional/Sports) ASDMARI also has training facilities for various martial art forms like Kick Boxing(Japanese Mix Form), Jiu-Jitsu(Japan), Tae Kwon Do(Korea), Aikido (Japan), Yoga(India), Krav Maga(Israel) and the use for oriental weapons to its members.
In 2015 ASDMARI Participated in Open National Championship in Chhatrishgarh and represented his home state West Bengal. In 2016 ASDMARI and NSKAWB participated in both Kata & Kumite in National Championship held in Nagpur, Maharashtra and got 3rd Runners Up position among 16 states. ASDMARI participated in Karate Association of India’s Referee & Judge Examination in 2017 in Nagpur and cleared with good grades.
Between 2014 – 2019 many students participated in District, State, and National Karate Championship, Camps and Examination and proved themselves with distinctive results. Most of the students are in good positions in his field. Some of them have become Professional Trainers in different types of martial arts. Karate is not only a mere sport and an act of self-defence nowadays but people are taking it up as a serious profession.

Our Mission

“What you have been taught by listening to others' words you will forget very quickly; what you have learned with your whole body you will remember for the rest of your life.” -- Gichin Funakoshi

Our Mission is to help you improve your personality and discipline through martial arts that will help in personal growth and development. We want to provide our students with several types of martial arts training that fit their personalities. Martial art is not only learning about defence tactics but it makes your body and mind stronger. As each community grows with positive changes, other communities can follow by mirroring what works, and thus, growing the goodness in human possibilities. As said by early 2nd century Latin Poet Juvenal “mens sana in corpore sano” (In Latin) which means “a healthy mind in a healthy body".

Shihan Swaprakash Roy - Founder ASDMARI

Shihan Swaprakash Roy a 4th Dan Black Belt and also the authorized judge and referee of the Karate Association of India (KAI) is a former National Champion. His has an experience of 24 years in different types of full contact Karate, Sports Karate, Tae Kwon Do, etc. Shihan Swaprakash Roy started his Martial Arts career in full contact karate under Renshi Mrinal Sen in 1995 at New West Bengal Martial Arts Society affiliated to Okinawa Martial Arts Academy, Japan.
Sensei Swaprakash Roy was trained under Sensei Sujit Salder, Sensei Swapan Patra, Sensei Molay Basu Roy, Sensei Ashis Mathur, Sensei Subir Sadhukhan, Shihan Bimal Debnath, Sifu Rupam Roy (Kung Fu), Amritamoy Das (Jeet Kwon Do & Wing Chun), etc. He also practices full contact Kyokushin, Traditional Shotokan Karate, Sports Karate (WKF).Shihan was also trained under some famous national and international teacher’s including Japan Karate Association (JKA) and Japan Karate Shotokan (JKS). In 1999 he was trained under Shihan Erik Govender & Silver Govender (South Africa), Sensei Somnath Pal Chowdhury, the Chief Technical Director of the Indian Federation of Shotokan Karate (IFSK), Shihan Sanjay Ingole, Chief Instructor and Technical Director National Shotokan Karate-Do Association India, Shihan Takashi Yamaguchi of JKS Shotokan-Ryu Karate-Do Association (Japan), Shihan Tadashi Ishikawa of JKS Shotokan-Ryu Karate-Do Association (Japan), Shihan Dave Hooper of Japan Karate Association (JKA), etc.
His passion for this art is indomitable. His constant efforts to re-invent easy self-defence techniques have transformed the academy into a Research unit.
